Why Participate

Top Students

Stanford is one of the top universities in the United States and enjoys worldwide prestige. Career Ch!na offers a unique opportunity for Chinese employers to meet and recruit students at Stanford.

While based at Stanford, our cooperation network is not limited to Stanford University. Career Ch!na provids you with a much bigger platform where you can attract talented students from all first-class universities in California. Through our Career Fair, you can efficiently promote your organization, collect resumes from top students, and screen excellent potential candidates.

Easy Overseas Hiring

Want to recruit outstanding American-educated Chinese students? Come to Career Ch!na! Hiring is that easy. In addition to the one-day Career Fair, we will design and organize evening reception/information session, welcome dinner and Carer Fair guidance. Also, we are well aware of your concerns as travelling recruiters, and we strive to accommodate your specific needs. You can try our optional one-stop Logistical Service Package which includes airport pick-up, hotel booking, and Stanford campus tour. The only thing you need to do is to bring your company's job openings and the criteria for future employers to Stanford!

Powerful Marketing and Branding

Plan to expand your business to overseas markets in the future or simply want to enhance your brand's global influence? We are the right people to talk to! The Silicon Valley is America's and the world's leading hub for high-tech innovation and development, and the Bay Area where Stanford is located is home to many largest technology corporations and most vibrant startups in the world. As our participant, your company and brand will be recognized in all our publicity and promotional materials. Throughout our service, you can meet future business leaders, exchange ideas with peer companies, make your stories heard and your brand known in the Silicon Valley.

Sustainable Platform

Career Ch!na aims to establish a sustainable platform for overseas recruitment and we value long-term cooperation. Our vision is to be the leading international career development organization providing the best service for matching overseas Chinese students and China-based companies. Join us today and invest in your company's future!
