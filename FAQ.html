Questions & Answers

  • Is Career Ch!na a company business program?

    Career Ch!na is a program organized by Association of Chinese Students and Scholars at Stanford (ACSSS). ACSSS is a non-profit organization, and Career Ch!na is also a non-profit program.

  • Who is our partner?

    Our partners currently include Stanford Career Development Center (CDC), and Chinese Students and Scholars Associations of UC Berkeley, Caltech, UCLA, UCSD, UCSB, UC Davis, and USC.

  • When is the Career Fair taking place?

    The Career Fair is scheduled from February 28-March 6, 2013, which includes a standard Stanford career fair procedure and an optional 3-day interview session. Detailed schedule is as below:

    Feb. 28: Companies Check-in and career fair instructions

    Mar. 1st: Stanford campus tour, Company info session and welcome networking dinner

    Mar. 2nd: Career Fair

    Mar. 3rd-Mar. 6: Optional interview session

  • Who will come to the Career Fair?

    Students from Stanford University, University of California, Berkeley, California Institute of Technology and other prestigious universities around Silicon Valley. Also, experienced Chinese employees who have been working in Silicon Valley will come to the event.

  • What service do Career Ch!na provide?

    We are dedicated to facilitate your recruiting and cultural experience here in the Silicon Valley. Based on specific needs of companies, we provide complimentary airport pick-up, hotel reservation, and Stanford campus tour. If you have any other questions or needs, please feel free to let us know.

We are constantly updating this list. If you have any questions, please contact us.
